At WellNest Clinics, we are committed to protecting the confidentiality and security of your personal and medical information. This registration form collects details such as your name, date of birth, contact information, insurance type, and medical history for the sole purpose of providing safe and effective healthcare services.

We will never sell or disclose your information to unauthorized third parties. Information may only be shared with healthcare providers directly involved in your care, or as required by law. All data is stored securely using industry‑standard safeguards, and access is restricted to authorized staff who are trained in patient privacy practices.

Your information is used to create and maintain accurate medical records, schedule appointments, process billing and insurance claims, and ensure continuity of care. We may also use non‑identifiable data for quality improvement, research, and reporting purposes, but such data cannot be traced back to you personally.

By completing this form, you acknowledge that you understand how your information will be used and protected. You have the right to request access to your records, ask for corrections, and withdraw consent for certain uses at any time. If you choose not to provide the required information, some services may be limited.

For questions about our privacy practices, or to exercise your rights under HIPAA and applicable laws, please contact our compliance office at privacy@wellnestclinics.com or call our main office during business hours.
